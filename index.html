Who we are

Wamaki Safaris is a reliable tour operator based in Nairobi with over 20 years’ experience in the tourism industry.

We offer the most prestigious safaris to the natural and man-made attractions all over Kenya. We pride ourselves in having a dedicated and highly skilled workforce that ensures customers’ needs are met.

Our safaris are carefully created to give maximum enjoyment and best value for money. Our objective is to give memorable safaris to all our clients by offering them the best services. We are based in the heart of the city, Nairobi - Kenya.

Vision
